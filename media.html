Op de jaarlijkse Folkloredag trok Stitching Dutch Heritage veel belangstelling met haar unieke collectie van authentieke Nederlandse borduurwerken. Bezoekers konden genieten van werken die vaak 50-60 jaar oud zijn en met de hand zijn gemaakt.

[English translation of the newspaper article will go here]

At the annual Folkloredag, Stitching Dutch Heritage attracted considerable attention with its unique collection of authentic Dutch embroidery works. Visitors were able to enjoy pieces that are often 50-60 years old and hand-stitched.

"It's wonderful to see how these old crafts come to life again and are appreciated by a new audience."

The collections are now also available to museum shops and heritage parks worldwide, each with a certificate of authenticity.

Onze Missie Our Mission

Onze missie is om Nederlands erfgoed te verbinden met de wereld. Wij leveren aan museumshops, erfgoedparken en culturele festivals unieke, met de hand gemaakte borduurwerken – altijd authentiek, duurzaam en professioneel gerestaureerd.

Our mission is to connect Dutch heritage with the world. We supply museum shops, heritage parks, and cultural festivals with unique, hand-stitched works – always authentic, sustainable, and professionally restored.
