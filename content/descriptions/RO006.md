# English Description

This embroidery depicts the motto 'Je Maintiendrai' ('I will maintain'), the official motto of the Kingdom of the Netherlands. The motto, originally from the House of Orange-Nassau, symbolises the determination and steadfastness of the Dutch nation. Through the centuries it has remained a central element in Dutch heraldry and state symbolism.
